About Me

Hi, I'm Xinzhi Lin, a third-year undergraduate student at the University of Science and Technology of China (USTC), majoring in Information and Computing Science at the College of Mathematical Sciences. My academic journey is driven by a deep curiosity about Computer Graphics and Mathematics, and their real-world applications.

During my time at university, I've developed a strong foundation in Mathematics, C++ programming, and academic research. Looking ahead, I am eager to transition my classroom knowledge into industry experience. Currently, I am preparing for the summer camp entrance exam, aiming to continue my graduate studies at USTC. When I'm not coding or studying, you can usually find me listening to music, solving the Rubik's Cube, or exploring the city.
